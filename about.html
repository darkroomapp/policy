Публікація подій

Безкоштовно або за плату

Більшість заходів публікується безкоштовно. Оплата потрібна лише якщо ти хочеш розмістити подію далеко наперед.

Умови прості: якщо до початку події залишилось 15 або менше днів - публікація безкоштовна. Якщо більше - за кожен додатковий день стягується невелика плата.
Безпека та модерація

Перевірені анкети, живі люди

Кожен профіль та кожна подія проходять перевірку адміністраторами перед публікацією. Фейки та порушники блокуються.

Find companions
for any event

Dark Room is a bot for meeting people who are going to the same events as you. Register, find an event, meet your crowd.

Open in Telegram →
How it works
1
Create a profile
Add 1-3 photos, your name, a short bio, and your city. Your profile is reviewed by moderators before going live.
2
Find an event
Browse upcoming events in your city or across the whole country. Register for the ones that interest you.
3
Like profiles
Browse profiles of other attendees at the same events and like the ones you'd like to meet. You only see people going to the same places.
4
Get a match
When the feeling is mutual, the bot shares both contacts and tells you which shared event brought you together.
Features
📅
Event catalog
Concerts, parties, exhibitions, festivals - all upcoming events in one place. Filtered by city.
💛
Find friends
Swipe-style matching among real event attendees. No random strangers - only people nearby.
➕
Create events
Organize and publish your own events. Free if the event starts within 15 days.
🔍
Auto-search
Don't know where to go? Mark "Looking for an event" for a specific date and find others in the same situation.
🔔
Reminders
The bot reminds you about your event 24 hours before - no more forgetting the time or address.
🌐
Two languages
Full interface in Ukrainian and English. Switch anytime in settings.
Publishing events

Free or paid

Most events are published for free. Payment is only required if you want to post an event well in advance.

Simple terms: if your event starts within 15 days - publication is free. If it's further out - a small fee applies per additional day.
Safety & moderation

Verified profiles, real people

Every profile and every event is reviewed by moderators before going live. Fakes and violators are blocked.
